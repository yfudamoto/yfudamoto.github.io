Yoshinobu “Yoshi” Fudamoto
Observational astronomer at Chiba University, exploring early galaxies with ALMA and JWST, and using gravitational lensing to resolve stars and structure in the distant Universe.onal astronomer basing in Chiba University, Japan.

I study how the first galaxies formed and evolved by combining JWST’s rest-frame optical/UV view with ALMA’s cold gas and dust tracers.
I also use strong lensing to probe individual stars and fast transients in galaxies billions of light-years away.

千葉大学特任助教・ALMAとJWSTを用いて初期宇宙（高赤方偏移）銀河の観測的研究、重力レンズを用いた遠方銀河の個別恒星についての研究を行っています。

Keywords: High-redshift galaxies (z ≳ 6) • ISM & dust • Lensed stars/transients
